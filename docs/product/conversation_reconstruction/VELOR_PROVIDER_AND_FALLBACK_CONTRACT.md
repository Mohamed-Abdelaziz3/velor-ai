# VELOR Provider and Fallback Contract

The provider receives only a bounded plan and allowed fact set. Its response is
claim-verified before use; malformed, timed-out, unauthenticated, rate-limited,
or rejected output uses the deterministic composer. The deterministic composer
also handles social, unclear, action and handoff turns without pretending that
they require a catalog fact.

This verification environment has a placeholder Groq credential. No real
provider prose was certified.
