# Merchant Recruitment and Interview Guide

## 1. Recruitment Criteria

**Inclusion Criteria (Target ICP):**
- Egyptian business with active sales through Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp.
- Measurable weekly inbound conversations (min. 150/week).
- At least one dedicated person (or owner) responsible for replies.
- A defined catalog with identifiable prices.
- Experiences repeated customer questions.
- Admits to losing or delaying conversations.
- Willing to show their real workflow over a screen share or in person.
- Willing to share anonymized conversation samples.
- Direct access to the decision-maker/owner.

**Exclusion Criteria:**
- Negligible message volume (<20 messages/week).
- No stable products or prices (e.g., custom service quotes only).
- Purely offline operation.
- Regulated medical or financial advice verticals.
- Unwilling to provide workflow evidence or commit time.
- Expectation of a "fully autonomous, 100% human-free AI."

## 2. 30–40 Minute Interview Program

### Introduction (2 mins)
*We are building a tool to organize customer messages and stop lost sales. We are not selling anything today; we want to see how you currently work to ensure we solve real problems.*

### Part 1: Business Context (5 mins)
- "What are your top-selling products right now?"
- "Roughly, what is your average order value?"
- "Which channels generate the most messages (FB, Insta, WhatsApp)?"
- "Who answers the messages? How big is the team?"
- "Roughly how many messages do you get on a busy day versus a slow day?"

### Part 2: Workflow Observation (15 mins)
*(Ask the merchant to share their screen or show their phone)*
- "Show me the last message you received from a new customer. How did you respond?"
- "If a customer asks for the price or specs of [Top Product], show me exactly where you copy that information from."
- "Show me a customer who said they would 'think about it'. How do you remember to follow up?"
- "Walk me through the exact steps you take when a customer says 'I want to buy this right now'."
- "Show me a conversation where you (the owner) had to step in and take over from an employee. Why?"

### Part 3: Recent Incidents (10 mins)
- "Show me the last time a customer bought from a competitor because your reply was delayed."
- "When was the last time a customer was given the wrong price or an outdated policy?"
- "Show me the last purchase that failed right at the end (e.g., during payment or address collection)."

### Part 4: Workarounds and Costs (5 mins)
- "You mentioned using [Labels/Notebook/Spreadsheet] to track orders. How much time does that take daily?"
- "How many sales do you estimate you lose a week because of forgotten follow-ups?"

### Part 5: Commitment (3 mins)
- "If we could organize this into a single queue that highlights exactly who is ready to buy and who needs a follow-up, would you be willing to test it for 14 days using a subset of your real traffic?"
- "We are currently pricing the pilot program. At what price point would this be an automatic 'yes', and at what point would you need to think seriously about it?"
