## Highest-leverage root causes

1. No explicit canonical ownership of commercial concepts and projections.
2. Legacy monolith paths coexist with newer service/router contracts.
3. Runtime environment and repository hygiene do not provide a reproducible product reality baseline.
4. Product/copy/terminology migration from ADAM AI to VELOR is incomplete.
